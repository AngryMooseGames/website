What I want to do with Physics is to make a fun building (sandbox) game for you and your friends to play. While it sounds simple, I want it to be powerful, with an advanced map editor ingame, super cool sandbox tools, and more, allowing modders to mess around with the game and make some cool stuff.

The game is in heavy development right now, and the steam page is coming soon! You can signup for playtesting here. There's also an official Discord you can join
